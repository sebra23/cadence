Features

Own your sound. Never pay a royalty again.

Royalty-free playlists built around your brand, steer up to 10,000 stores from one dashboard, and automatically create more of what drives sales.

AI Music Generation

Your brand gets its own sound. Unlimited original tracks that are never repeated, never shared, and never mistaken for anyone else.

Multi-Location Control

You run every store from one place. Set the sound, shift the vibe, and keep every location on brand without touching a single one individually.

Traffic-Adaptive Playlists

Your music moves with your customers. Slower when the morning crowd eases in, building through the afternoon, peaking when your floor is at its busiest.

New Music Weekly

Every week, fresh tracks built from what your sales data says actually works — more of what keeps shoppers spending, automatically.

Zero Royalties

You own every track outright — no PRO fees, no licensing bills, no audits. What you save goes straight back into your business.

How It Works

You’re live across every store, restaurant, and hotel in four steps.

01

Brand Discovery

You tell us who you are. Connect with a sonic strategist and complete our Sonic Identity form and we translate your brand into music library with playlists that's entirely your own.

02

Find Your Sound

You choose what feels right. We generate custom sample tracks built around your brand profile. You listen, you pick, and your signature sound is locked in.

03

Connect Your Stores

You plug in your data or link your POS in real time so we can adjust tempo, energy, and scheduling to match your crowd automatically.

04

Go Live

You launch. Every store, every location, every time zone playing adaptive, royalty-free music that updates in real time and gets smarter about driving sales every single day.
